Projects
FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) is an international robotics competition where students design, build, and program robots to complete specific tasks. It helps them develop skills in engineering, teamwork, and problem-solving, encouraging interest in STEM fields.
Your sponsorship helps us meet important needs such as robot parts, competition entry fees, team travel expenses and educational materials.
Sponsoring our team boosts your brand visibility, demonstrates your support for STEM education, and connects you with future innovators. For more details, visit our support page.
